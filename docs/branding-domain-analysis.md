## Domain Workaround Note

From the June 11 meeting, the partners already discussed three paths when a preferred domain is taken:

1. **Buy from the squatter.** `candi.com` parks on abovedomains (a domain-holding registrar). `cinct.com` is listed on Afternic (GoDaddy's aftermarket). Afternic prices are typically $1K–$50K+ depending on the name. Negotiate via Afternic's "Buy Now" or make-offer flow. For candi.com, contact abovedomains directly or use a broker.

2. **Use a clean prefix/suffix variant.** `candi.io` and `knoq.io` are unregistered — a `.io` domain is common for developer-friendly B2B tools and carries no stigma in this space. Alternatively, `usecandi.ai` appears unregistered.

3. **Operate under a variant, lock the primary for later.** Register `knoq.io` today (~$40/yr), operate there, and acquire `knoq.com` once revenue supports it. This is standard for early-stage SaaS.
